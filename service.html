The challenge is not alone the mass penetration by these devices (both tablets & smartphones) but also the ever increasing variety in screen specifications, processor, memory, power consumptions and so on makes it a tough for any entity thinking of going mobile.

The mobile revolution not only opened doors for newer & smarter "Apps" but forced organizations to migrate millions of applications both enterprise & consumer based, both web & desktop based, to be made available on mobile. Therefore there are multiple roadblocks in front of organizations:

How to make our existing applications available on mobile with minimal changes?

How to develop applications to cater to wide variety of devices available in market?

How to propagate a small change in enterprise application to all mobile devices with just single click?

How to ensure security & data protection over mobile networks?

How to keep enhancing the overall application usability with ever changing OS, Hardware & screen sizes on these devices?

How to keep up the application pace with the newer versions/releases of mobile operating systems?
